About The Big Five

The Big Five website offer information about the famous big five game animals in Africa Safari. They include, the lion, leopard, black rhinoceros, African bush elephant, and the African buffalo. It contains facts about the different animals in a multimedia environment making use of images, videos articles.

Feel free to use and share the information gained from this site. Enjoy!
